Swift Camp

Wed July 13, 2016

@ United Nations

An open source, community-run and not-for-profit conference focused on the Swift programming language

SwiftCamp has two sessions - a small daytime TRAINING session and large cross-community evening TECH TALKS session.

Limited seats available for the daytime event. Please make sure you register below to secure your seat.

The training session targets absolute beginners. You will learn to work with XCode to build your first App with Swift.
All attendees are required to bring laptops (and chargers).

Both sessions will be hosted at the United Nations HQ in NYC

Please send us an email at info@swiftcamp.io if you have any questions or want to get involved.
